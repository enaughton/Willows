About

We offer an array of products from Bagels by the dozen to great coffee and tasty snack in between. A place to enjoy local handmade bagels and a place to enjoy friends

Find us at 71 South Union Street in Burlington, VT! We are conveniently located behind City Market, and share a building with Union Street Laundry, Folino's Pizza and Stonecutter Spirits. Parking is available in our shared lot.
